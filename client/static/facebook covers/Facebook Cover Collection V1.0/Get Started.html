Getting Started
You'll need Adobe Photoshop CS* to customize your product mockup.


Customization
Open the .PSD file and get set to edit your cover.

When you open the document you'll see a bunch of green Smart layers named "Photo #*". Double click the thumbnail of this layer to open the Smart object.
Now, paste in your own photograph, save and close the window. - Repeat this process for all image layers

Your cover is now customized. Easy as that!
If you feel for it, you can play around with stuff to your liking. There is no limit to what can be done!


Saving
Hold CTRL and SHIFT while clicking the "S" key and save as whatever format you'd like. (We recommend .jpg)
